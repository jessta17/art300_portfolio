Greeting Cards

Completed fall of 2019
Two greating cards were created for Halloween. They were inspired by an interview with a fellow classmate. The black card has foil on the cover and back. They were created through illustrator and indesign. The cards were cut using a laser cutter and then photographed using a professional camera. The medium is ink and paper
