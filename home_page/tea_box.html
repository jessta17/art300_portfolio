Tea Box

Completed fall of 2019
For this project a logotype and tea box were designed for a fictional company Helina. The designer had to come up with a tea flavor as well as the design for the packaging and logotype. The primary tools used were indesign and illustrator. The photos were taken using a professional camera. It is made out of coverstock in and lots of double sided tape. The inside of the box is also foiled.
